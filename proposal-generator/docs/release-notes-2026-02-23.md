# Prop Tart Release Notes

## Publish Microsite
You can now publish any proposal as a polished, shareable web page. Hit "Publish Microsite" in the top bar, get a clean URL, send it to your prospect. No login or download required on their end.

**What they see:**
- Full-screen branded cover page
- "Future of HR" vision section framing the transformation opportunity
- Executive summary with animated key metrics
- Meet Harper with capabilities and proof points
- Value drivers tailored to their pain points
- Investment case with year-by-year breakdown
- **Interactive ROI Explorer** — prospects toggle between Conservative, Recommended, and Optimistic scenarios
- Security & integration landscape showing their stack
- Next steps with your contact info

Re-publishing updates the same URL. Works on any device.

## Quick ROI Estimate
The ROI calculator now has a **quick mode**. Answer 4 questions (employee count, industry, workforce type, org model) and Prop Tart generates a full ROI estimate using industry benchmarks. You can refine from there or use the numbers as-is. Great for early-stage deals where you don't have detailed inputs yet.

For smaller accounts (<2,500 employees), Prop Tart automatically emphasizes compliance risk reduction and manager time savings instead of headcount reduction — a more realistic story for that segment.

## Expanded ROI Inputs
For detailed mode, the ROI calculator now supports:
- **Manager HR time savings** — capture how much time managers spend fielding HR questions
- **Triage role savings** — model the cost of dedicated triage FTEs
- **Salary regions** — adjust for multi-geography cost structures
- **Audit prep savings** — time saved on compliance audit preparation
- **Risk pattern detection** and **proactive alerts** value — compliance upside beyond cost avoidance
- **Seepage and overhead multipliers** — fine-tune the model

## Customer Quotes Library
New **Customer Quotes** tab in the form. Select from real customer quotes organized by proposal section (Executive Summary, Meet Harper, Value Drivers, etc.). Selected quotes appear inline in the proposal document at the right section.

## FAQs Section
New **FAQs** tab lets you add anticipated questions and answers, organized by proposal page. These render as "Anticipated Questions" blocks within each relevant section of the proposal. You can also auto-generate FAQs from deal context and Gong notes.

## Form & Config Improvements
- **Custom "Other" options** — Industry, contact title, and all integration dropdowns now support "Other" with a free-text field (no more forcing prospects into predefined buckets)
- **Custom pain points** — Add your own beyond the preset list, with drag-to-reorder
- **Custom next steps** — Same flexibility for proposed next steps
- **Pain point & next step ordering** — Drag to reorder how they appear in the proposal
- Agent engineering pricing is now optional and cleaner in the config
- Annual escalation for multi-year contracts is now optional

## DOCX Export Improvements
The Word export now includes all the new sections and handles the expanded data model (custom pain points, customer quotes, FAQs, resolved "Other" values for integrations).
