1. ORGANIZATION OVERVIEW

[Confidential Organization] is a rapidly growing company with approximately 2,000 employees across multiple U.S. locations. The People Operations function supports a diverse workforce and includes HR Business Partners, Shared Services, Employee Relations, Total Rewards, and HRIS.

The HR team is currently experiencing high volumes of transactional inquiries, manual process administration, and inconsistent intake workflows. We are seeking an AI-enabled HR Operations Automation Platform to improve efficiency, reduce service delivery costs, mitigate compliance risk, and enhance the employee experience.

2. OBJECTIVE

The objective of this RFP is to identify and select a vendor that can deliver an AI-powered HR operations solution capable of:

- Automating Tier 0 and Tier 1 employee inquiries
- Reducing service ticket volume and manual case handling
- Guiding managers through compliant decision-making processes
- Integrating seamlessly with Workday
- Providing operational visibility and analytics

3. CURRENT STATE CHALLENGES

Vendors should address how their solution would improve the following:

- High volume of repetitive HR inquiries (policy questions, benefits, PTO, payroll timing)
- Manual leave of absence intake and documentation collection
- Manager reliance on HRBPs for performance documentation guidance
- HRIS data corrections and transaction processing delays
- Fragmented intake across Slack, email, and ticketing systems
- Limited analytics into case trends, policy gaps, and deflection rates

4. SCOPE OF REQUIREMENTS

4.1 AI HR Inquiry Automation
- Natural language understanding for policy and benefits questions
- Context-aware responses using company-specific documentation
- Ability to escalate to HR when appropriate
- Audit trail of interactions

4.2 Workflow & Process Automation
- Guided workflows for leave intake, performance management support, job changes, and onboarding tasks
- Intelligent document collection and validation
- Automated routing to appropriate stakeholders
- Structured case intake and tracking

4.3 Manager Enablement
- Real-time guidance for performance concerns, corrective actions, and documentation best practices
- Policy-aligned recommendations
- Standardized templates and documentation support

4.4 HRIS & Systems Integration
- Must integrate with Workday (required)
- Ability to initiate or facilitate Workday transactions (e.g., job changes, PTO updates, data corrections)
- API-based architecture preferred
- SSO support (Okta or similar)

4.5 Communication Channel Integration
- Deployment within Slack and/or Microsoft Teams
- Email support
- Optional integration with ServiceNow or similar ticketing systems

4.6 Compliance & Risk Management
- Policy-consistent responses
- Configurable approval flows
- Secure data handling and role-based permissions
- SOC 2 or equivalent certification preferred

4.7 Analytics & Reporting
- Dashboard showing deflection rates
- Case volume trends and topic clustering
- SLA tracking
- ROI reporting (time saved, cost avoidance estimates)

5. IMPLEMENTATION REQUIREMENTS

- Implementation timeline under 12 weeks preferred
- Clear onboarding and configuration process
- Dedicated implementation and customer success support
- Change management best practices included

6. VENDOR RESPONSE REQUIREMENTS

Vendors should provide:

- Company overview and client references
- Description of AI architecture and approach to data security
- Integration details specific to Workday
- Implementation methodology and timeline
- Pricing structure (subscription-based preferred)
- Case studies demonstrating measurable impact (e.g., deflection rate improvements, cost savings)

7. EVALUATION CRITERIA

Proposals will be evaluated based on:

- Depth of HR operations automation capability
- Strength of Workday integration
- Demonstrated ROI and measurable outcomes
- Security and compliance posture
- User experience for employees and managers
- Total cost of ownership
